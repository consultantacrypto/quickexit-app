## Problem

Owners of real-world assets — vehicles, businesses, property, luxury goods, equipment — often need **speed over maximum price**. Classifieds lack trust and structure; brokers are slow and costly. Sellers under time pressure and buyers with deployable capital both lack a **purpose-built liquidity layer** with verified identity, AI-guided pricing, and structured offer flows.

---

## Solution

**Quick Exit** is a live digital marketplace that connects sellers who need rapid exits with buyers seeking time-sensitive or below-market opportunities. The platform facilitates discovery, verification, and negotiation. **Quick Exit does not hold funds in custody** or intermediate payments between parties.

**Core flows:** seller listings · buyer capital demands · fixed exit-price offers · open auctions · negotiation workspace

**Categories:** Auto & Moto · Real Estate · Luxury · Businesses · Gadgets · Photo & Audio

## Trust — Didit KYC

Identity verification via **Didit** (hosted flow, webhook status mapping). KYC supports counterparty trust on high-value asset transactions. Stripe handles platform fees only — not buyer/seller settlement.

---

## AI — HQ Copilot

**HQ Copilot** gives operators structured intelligence over marketplace activity: listings, demands, offers, KYC profiles, and risk signals. Modes include daily snapshot, risk review, priorities, and growth. **Evaluation API** guides sellers on asset pricing during onboarding. AI recommends; humans approve compliance actions.
