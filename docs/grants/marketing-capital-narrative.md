## 7. KPIs — Marketplace Liquidity (Not Ad Metrics)

| KPI | Definition | 90-day target (grant reporting) |
|-----|------------|--------------------------------|
| **Cost per seller listing started** | Ad spend ÷ `start_post_listing` events | Establish baseline; improve 20% |
| **Cost per active listing** | Ad spend ÷ listings reaching `active` status | Establish baseline |
| **Cost per buyer demand** | Ad spend ÷ `start_post_demand` events | Establish baseline |
| **Cost per offer submitted** | Ad spend ÷ `submit_listing_offer` + `submit_demand_offer` | Establish baseline |
| **KYC completion rate** | Verified profiles ÷ registered users from paid cohorts | ≥ 25% |
| **Offer-to-view ratio** | Offers ÷ `view_listing` from paid traffic | ≥ 3% |
| **Liquidity depth** | Active listings + active demands (monthly) | Month-over-month growth |

**Explicitly exclude as primary KPIs:** impressions, likes, followers, "brand awareness" without funnel linkage.

## 8. Controlled Experiment Protocol

Grant reviewers want discipline. Include this protocol:

| Phase | Duration | Spend cap | Success criteria |
|-------|----------|-----------|------------------|
| **Pilot** | 14 days | 20% of monthly budget | ≥1 liquidity event per $50 spend |
| **Validate** | 14 days | 50% of monthly budget | CPA at or below pilot benchmark |
| **Scale** | 30 days | 30% of monthly budget | Scale only validated cohorts |
| **Kill** | Immediate | — | Cohort CPA >2× benchmark after 500 clicks |

All experiments tagged in GA4 with `utm_campaign`, `utm_source`, `utm_medium` per `docs/analytics-events.md` attribution fields.
